Profile

I am an urban planning and spatial data student interested in city regeneration, transport planning, urban spatial analysis and evidence-led design. My work combines planning knowledge with digital and analytical tools, including CAD, SketchUp, Photoshop, Illustrator, ArcGIS, QGIS, SQL, R, Python, NetLogo, machine learning and data mining.

I have a strong understanding of UK planning systems, development processes and site assessment, with experience producing clear recommendations for practical and operational decisions. I am a diligent, adaptable and collaborative learner, with experience coordinating teamwork and contributing within multidisciplinary planning, design and transport environments.
